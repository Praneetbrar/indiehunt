## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: Who can submit projects to IndieHunt?**  
A: Anyone who has built an AI project or digital product can submit. You'll need to create an account, have a working website URL, and provide accurate project information.

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: Click "Sign In" and choose from three methods: email (magic link), Google OAuth, or GitHub OAuth. It's free and takes seconds.

**Q: What information do I need to submit?**  
A: Required: project name, website URL, descriptions, categories, logo, and preview image. Optional: screenshots, video, maker info. Our AI autofill can extract most information automatically.

### Launch Types & Pricing

**Q: What's the difference between Free, Nofollow, and Premium launches?**  
A: **Free Launch**: Free, requires badge on website, nofollow by default (dofollow if you win). **Nofollow Launch**: Free, no badge required, always nofollow. **Premium Launch**: $10, guaranteed dofollow, no badge, priority placement, newsletter feature.

**Q: Can I change my launch type after submission?**  
A: Yes, but only before your project is scheduled. Once approved and scheduled, launch type cannot be changed.

**Q: Can I relaunch my project if it doesn't win?**  
A: Yes! If your project completes a launch week and doesn't place in top 3, you can relaunch once for $10. Your project is upgraded to Premium, all links become dofollow, and existing votes are preserved.

### Weekly Competition

**Q: How does the weekly competition work?**  
A: All projects scheduled for a week launch simultaneously on Monday at 8:00 AM UTC. The competition runs for 7 days, ending the following Monday at 7:59 AM UTC. Top 3 projects by votes win.

**Q: How are winners determined?**  
A: Winners are determined purely by vote count. The project with the most votes wins first place, second most wins second place, and third most wins third place.

**Q: What do winners receive?**  
A: All top 3 winners receive: exclusive winner badge, badge assets for their website, Hall of Fame placement. Free & Premium launch winners also receive: automatic upgrade to dofollow backlinks, enhanced SEO benefits.

**Q: Can I vote for my own project?**  
A: Yes! We encourage creators to share their projects and get votes from their network. Authentic engagement from real users is what matters most.

### Backlinks & SEO

**Q: What's the difference between nofollow and dofollow backlinks?**  
A: **Nofollow links** tell search engines not to pass SEO value (still provide traffic). **Dofollow links** pass SEO authority and can improve search rankings. Both provide traffic, but dofollow has additional SEO benefits.

**Q: How do I verify my backlink for Free launches?**  
A: Add any link to indiehunt.io on your website (footer, about page, etc.). Our system automatically verifies it by checking your website's source code. You can use our provided badge code or a simple text link.

**Q: Where will my backlink appear?**  
A: Your backlink appears on: project detail page, homepage listings, project browse page, and related projects sections. All links point to your website URL.

### Payment & Premium

**Q: How does Premium launch payment work?**  
A: Select "Premium Launch" during submission, complete project information, and proceed to payment. You'll be redirected to our secure payment processor (Polar) to complete the $10 one-time payment. After payment, your project is automatically scheduled.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and other payment methods supported by Polar. Payments are secure, encrypted, and PCI-compliant.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Premium launch payments are generally non-refundable. However, refunds may be considered for technical errors, service non-delivery, duplicate payments, or cancellation requests made before processing. Contact support for specific circumstances.

### Dashboard & Tracking

**Q: What can I see in my dashboard?**  
A: Your dashboard shows: all your submissions (drafts, pending, scheduled, live, past), current status of each project, vote counts and rankings, launch dates, and project performance metrics.

**Q: Can I edit my project after submission?**  
A: Yes, but with limitations. You can edit draft submissions and scheduled projects (before they go live). You cannot edit website URL or launch type, or projects that are currently live.

**Q: How do I track votes and performance?**  
A: Your dashboard shows real-time vote counts, ranking position during competitions, status updates, and you'll receive email notifications for key events.
